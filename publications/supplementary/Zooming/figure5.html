IEEE VIS 2015 - Towsey et al - Figure 5

A sequence of eleven images from smallest scale (top) to largest scale (bottom) focused on minute 600 (10am). The transition from image 8 (0.2s/px) to image 9 (0.1s/px) is the transition from false-color BGN-POW-CVR spectrograms to the pseudo-color cubehelix representation. An interactive zooming implementation of this image is at http://www.ecosounds.org/VIS15/zoom.

A focused stack of false-color spectrogram with varying resolutions.

Axes: The x axis time scale varies from small-scale (top) to large scale (bottom). The scales shown are: 60, 24, 12, 6, 2, 1, 0.6, 0.2, 0.1, 0.04, 0.02 seconds/pixel. The horizontal grid-lines are at 1 kHz intervals.
